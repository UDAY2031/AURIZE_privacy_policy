System Logs: Privacy Protocol

AURIZE Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 30, 2026

Welcome to AURIZE ("the App"). AURIZE is a fitness RPG system designed to help Players track their physical evolution. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the System.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Camera Data

AURIZE utilizes your device's camera specifically for the Exercise Tracking feature (Push-ups and Squats).

  • Purpose: The System uses computer vision to detect body movements and verify that the Player has completed the assigned repetitions.
  • Storage: We do not record, store, or transmit any video or image data to our servers. All processing happens locally and in real-time on your device.

1.2 Location Data

AURIZE utilizes your device's GPS and location services for the Running Tracker and Leaderboard features.

  • Tracking: We access "Fine Location" to calculate distance, speed, and duration of your quests. This only occurs while the App is active.
  • Leaderboard: The System periodically updates your last known coordinates to show other Players in your immediate vicinity (Nearby mode).
  • Requirement: Fine location is necessary for the accuracy of movement synchronization.

2. Data Storage (Firebase)

We use Google Firebase to ensure your progression is synchronized across the System:

  • Authentication: Your email and chosen username are stored securely via Firebase Authentication.
  • Player Status: Levels, Rank (E-Rank to National Level), XP, and Health status are saved in Firestore.
  • System Analytics: We use technical logs to identify bugs and improve System stability.

3. Data Security

The System prioritizes the protection of Player data using industry-standard encryption and Firebase security protocols. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute invulnerability.

4. Children's Privacy

AURIZE is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover a Player under 13 has provided data, it will be immediately deleted.
